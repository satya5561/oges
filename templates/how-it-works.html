Coride is a network of friendly people who share rides from one place to another. Here's how it works.

Search

Take a look at our map to find trips to the places you want to be.

You can also submit a trip request, browse profiles, or chat privately with other coriders.

Book

Request your booking in a snap and you're good to go!

Coride uses Stripe.com to secure your payments. Secure and hassle-free.

Coride

Meet up with your corider, fistbump, and enjoy the ride!

Feel free to bring snacks, a positive attitude, and great music with you on every coride.
